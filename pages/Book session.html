1-on-1 Soccer Goalie Training

Welcome to Maxx's goalie training program! Whether you're a beginner or an experienced goalkeeper looking to sharpen your skills, 1-on-1 training sessions can help you develop the techniques and confidence needed to excel in the game.

What Youll Learn

  • Proper diving techniques to make critical saves
  • Footwork and positioning for better goal coverage
  • Hand-eye coordination drills
  • Shot-stopping and reflex improvement
  • Game awareness and decision-making skills

Why 1-on-1 Training?

Personalized coaching allows goalkeepers to focus on their unique strengths and weaknesses, receiving direct feedback and tailored drills to enhance their performance.
